Group buys, in-stock parts, and vendors

Group buys are pre-orders for custom keyboards and keysets that are made-to-order in batch sales. The process starts with an interest check, followed by a limited-time pre-order period lasting 2-4 weeks or more, then production which can take 6-12 months with potential delays. Customers receive their products, and optional phases like extras or R2 may follow. Nowadays, there are companies with in-stock products making it easier to acquire keyboard accessories. See list of vendors selling keyboards and accessories. these are only some of the vendors that are available, notice that many stores deliver worldwide so for now I will focus on 10 that I know deliver to the US and the same with Canada, more stores will be added in the future. If you'd like to see everything that is in stock right now ThocStock is a great place to do that, and if you want to see if there are any group buys going on right now; mechgroupbuys would be the place to go to.
